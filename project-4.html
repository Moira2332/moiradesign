The Immatation Game

This peice is a quote book. I chose The Imitation Game, for this quote book, because of my admiration and respect for Alin Turing. I wanted to focus on his relationship with Joan Clarke. I found she brought something to his very stable existence that shook him up and brought colour to his world. I have decided to use a white page with a spacious design using a font such as Futura for Alan but adding watercolor and a script font done by hand for Joan. My design will be somewhat miss matched but I plan to find a way to make the contrasting fonts cohesive through having strict space sizing and a fluent spacious pattern with the text.

Process

In this process of creating this project I found alot of book or papers written by Alan. I then decided to study his writing and try to copy it. I then added his writing and my interpritation of Joans writing into the book. To keep it from overwleming the book I also included type. Throughout the movie Alan becomes more and more unwell. I attempted to show this through his writing. The more unwell his was the more scattered his writing became.
